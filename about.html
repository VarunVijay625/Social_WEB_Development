The web is a brand new way to map out the social sphere at UVM! Inherent to a college campus is that people are interconnected in ways you would not expect. Your chemistry lab partner may be on an intramural sports team with your roommate. Your classmate might have met your best friend at an ice-cream social. Da Web is your vehicle to map out and visualize the connections that occur etween us in a fun, accessible, and interactive way.
